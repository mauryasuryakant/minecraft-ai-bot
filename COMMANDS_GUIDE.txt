==================================================
MINECRAFT AI BOT - COMMANDS GUIDE
==================================================

This guide lists all available commands for the Minecraft AI Companion Bot.
The default prefix is '!'.

--------------------------------------------------
BASIC COMMANDS
--------------------------------------------------

!status
- Description: Shows the bot's current health, hunger level, and coordinates.
- Usage: !status

!come
- Description: Makes the bot walk to your current position.
- Usage: !come

!eat
- Description: Forces the bot to check its inventory for food and eat if hungry.
- Usage: !eat
- Note: The bot also has an 'auto-eat' feature enabled by default.

!sleep
- Description: The bot will look for a nearby bed and attempt to sleep in it.
- Usage: !sleep

!drop
- Description: The bot will drop all items currently in its inventory.
- Usage: !drop

!stop
- Description: Immediately stops any current task (mining, moving, following, guarding).
- Usage: !stop

!inventory (or !inv)
- Description: Lists all items currently in the bot's inventory.
- Usage: !inventory

!guard
- Description: The bot will follow you and automatically attack nearby hostile mobs.
- Usage: !guard

!torch
- Description: Places a torch at the bot's current feet position if it's dark.
- Usage: !torch

!pos
- Description: Reports the bot's current coordinates.
- Usage: !pos

!xp
- Description: Shows the bot's experience level and progress.
- Usage: !xp

!home [set]
- Description: Returns to the set home position. Use '!home set' to mark a new home.
- Usage: !home set
- Usage: !home

!lookat
- Description: Makes the bot look directly at the player.
- Usage: !lookat

!give [item] [amount]
- Description: Tosses a specified item and amount to you.
- Usage: !give cobble 64

!loot
- Description: Bot will walk to and pick up the nearest item on the ground.
- Usage: !loot

!equip [item]
- Description: Manually equips an item in the appropriate slot (armor or hand).
- Usage: !equip iron_chestplate

!deposit
- Description: Deposits all non-essential items (not tools/food) into the nearest chest.
- Usage: !deposit

!fish
- Description: Starts an automatic fishing loop. Use !stop to quit.
- Usage: !fish

!farm
- Description: Harvests nearby fully grown wheat, carrots, or potatoes.
- Usage: !farm

!say [message]
- Description: Makes the bot repeat your message in the chat.
- Usage: !say Hello everyone!

!smelt
- Description: Opens a nearby furnace and puts in raw ores and fuel.
- Usage: !smelt

--------------------------------------------------
MOVEMENT & NAVIGATION
--------------------------------------------------

!goto X Y Z
- Description: Moves the bot to the specified coordinates.
- Usage: !goto 100 64 -200

!follow
- Description: Starts following the player who issued the command.
- Usage: !follow

--------------------------------------------------
MINING & RESOURCE COLLECTION
--------------------------------------------------

!mine [BLOCK_NAME]
- Description: Finds the nearest block of the specified type and mines it.
- Usage: !mine diamond_ore
- Note: Automatically equips the best tool.

!collect [BLOCK_NAME]
- Description: Finds up to 5 nearby blocks of the specified type and collects them.
- Usage: !collect oak_log

!stripmine [DIRECTION] [DISTANCE]
- Description: Starts a 1x2 strip mine tunnel in a specific direction.
- Directions: north, south, east, west.
- Usage: !stripmine north 30

!scan [BLOCK_NAME]
- Description: Scans the area (64 block radius) for a specific block and reports if found.
- Usage: !scan iron_ore

!findvillage
- Description: Scans for a nearby village (searching for bells or villagers within 128 blocks).
- Usage: !findvillage

!guide [TARGET]
- Description: Leads you to a target (village or block name). The bot will wait for you if you fall behind.
- Usage: !guide village
- Usage: !guide diamond_ore
- Note: You must be nearby when issuing the command.

--------------------------------------------------
TIPS & TROUBLESHOOTING
--------------------------------------------------
- If the bot is stuck, use !stop.
- Ensure the bot has food in its inventory for auto-eating.
- Strip mining requires the bot to have pickaxes/tools for best results.
